Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers! Here are some of the most common queries from our users.

How do I add products to my cart?

Simply browse through our products, select your preferred item, choose quantity or size if applicable, and click the “Add to Cart” button.

Can I edit my cart before checkout?

Yes! You can adjust quantities or remove items anytime before confirming your order on the Cart page.

What technologies power SmartShop?

SmartShop is built using a combination of modern web technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS to ensure a seamless shopping experience.

What inspired the creation of SmartShop?

The idea came from wanting to build a simple yet powerful e-commerce platform that combines speed, trust, and a delightful user interface.
